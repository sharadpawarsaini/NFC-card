About NFCure

NFCure is an AI-powered emergency health passport that allows users to store and share their medical information instantly using QR codes and NFC tags. Our mission is to save lives by ensuring critical health data is always accessible during emergencies.

Our Vision

To create a world where no emergency treatment is delayed due to missing information.

Our Technology

We use secure cloud storage, QR/NFC sharing, encryption, and AI-based risk analysis.
